The Loop
Eat. Feel. Learn. Repeat.
Most food apps tell you what to eat. Antiflam tells you what your body actually reacts to.
1

Scan your food

Point your camera at a meal, ingredient, or barcode. AI breaks down every item and scores it 0–10 for inflammation.

2

Log it

One tap saves it to your journal with the time, meal type, and score. Or type it in manually — we score that too.

3

Check in later

Two hours after you eat, a gentle ping asks: how do you feel? Bloated? Brain fog? Great? Rate it 1–5 and note symptoms.

4

See what's yours

After a week, the insights engine shows your personal triggers. Not generic advice — patterns from your food and your body.
